"Hello everyone! My name is Shubhansh Shrivastava and I'm the center coordinator of LiGHT Gwalior.I'm going to share a short story of mine about how all this has been started and how I reached this position.I was running an NGO in my college for 6 months which was not registered. ... I just had a plan and a mission. I was seeking a platform to get guidance and sources for better organization.Later one of my classmates suggested me to join LiGHT. Then I went through their official site and found it very helpful. LIGHT is helping students who have an urge to run an NGO but don't have proper guidance.Then I joined this organization with my old team. We've transformed everything as per LIGHT IIT Kharagpur Chapter's suggestions. We also did many tasks which helped us to improve our data briefing and presentation skill.Currently, I'm working with LIGHT positively and also designing plans for our center.
